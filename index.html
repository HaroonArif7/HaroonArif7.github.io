01 — About

Background & approach

// haroon_arif.jpg HA replace with photo

I'm a data scientist who cares less about the fanciest model and more about whether the answer is correct, explainable, and shipped. My work usually starts with a messy CSV or a badly documented database, and ends with something a non-technical stakeholder can act on with confidence.

Over the past few years I've worked across predictive modelling, NLP, and business analytics, moving between exploratory notebooks and production pipelines. I'm most interested in problems where the data is imperfect and the stakes are real.

Outside of client and project work, I write about applied machine learning and keep a running set of experiments — some of which turn into the case studies below.
